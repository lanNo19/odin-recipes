Lasagna

Ingredients:

Bolognese
  • Ground Beef: 0.8 kg
  • Onion & Garlic: 3 pcs
  • Carrot: 3 pcs
  • Tomato Paste / Crushed: 0.6 kg

Additionally:

  • Salt & Pepper
  • Italian Spices
  • Basil
Bechamele
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt & Pepper

Other Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Do the Bolognese Sauce
  2. Do the Bechamele Sauce
  3. Prepare an oven to 180oC
  4. Prepare cooking tray by using butter
  5. Lay the first layer of bolognese
  6. Add parmesan
  7. Add bechamele
  8. Add pasta
  9. Repeat from step 5
  10. When you want to finish, lay down bolognese and top it up with cheese
  11. Done. Great Job!

Now, let your Lasagna cool down a little, and it is ready to be served
